Nintendo Switch
The Nintendo Switch, released in March 2017, is a hybrid gaming console that can be used both as a home console and a portable device. It features detachable Joy-Con controllers, allowing for versatile multiplayer gameplay. The Switch supports a diverse library of games, including major titles like "The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild," "Super Mario Odyssey," and "Animal Crossing: New Horizons." Its unique design and broad appeal helped it achieve significant commercial success, making it one of Nintendo's best-selling consoles. The Switch also emphasizes digital gaming and online connectivity, further enhancing its popularity.
